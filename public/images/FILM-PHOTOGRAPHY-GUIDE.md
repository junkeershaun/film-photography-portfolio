# Film Photography Portfolio - Image Guide

## Required Images

To complete your film photography portfolio, add these images to this folder:

### 1. Hero Background (`hero-bg.jpg`)
- **Purpose**: Main background for the hero section
- **Recommended**: A striking film photograph (landscape or abstract)
- **Size**: 1920x1080px or larger
- **Style**: High contrast, dramatic lighting

### 2. Profile Image (`profile.jpg`)
- **Purpose**: Your photo in the About section
- **Recommended**: You with a camera, in a darkroom, or a professional portrait
- **Size**: 800x800px (square)
- **Style**: Should reflect your personality as a photographer

### 3. Gallery Images

#### `project1.jpg` - Urban Moments Series
- **Subject**: Street photography
- **Style**: Black and white, high contrast
- **Film stock**: Kodak Tri-X 400 aesthetic
- **Size**: 1200x800px (landscape)

#### `project2.jpg` - Portraits on Film Series
- **Subject**: Portrait photography
- **Style**: Natural tones, warm colors
- **Film stock**: Kodak Portra 400 aesthetic
- **Size**: 1200x800px (landscape or portrait)

#### `project3.jpg` - Landscapes Series
- **Subject**: Landscape/nature photography
- **Style**: Medium format look, rich detail
- **Film stock**: Ilford HP5 aesthetic
- **Size**: 1200x800px (landscape)

## Image Optimization Tips

1. **Export at high quality** - Film photography deserves it
2. **Keep some grain** - It's part of the aesthetic
3. **Don't over-sharpen** - Film has natural softness
4. **Maintain aspect ratios** - Consistent sizing looks professional
5. **Use JPEG format** - Good balance of quality and file size
